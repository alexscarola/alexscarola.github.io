About Me

If you are here looking for my resume, feel free to click the folder below to download it. Otherwise, keep reading to get to know more about me.

My name is Alexandra Scarola, and I am a graduating senior from the University of Colorado Boulder. I am majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Education. I am currently a software developer at Tolstoy Plant-Based Eatery. We were previously working on creating an online food ordering application for IOS and Android, but now moved onto creating an application for training employees. I am mostly known for my work with frontend design, but am always interested in gaining opportunities in other sectors of computer science. Always an opportunity for growth!

One thing about myself is I am always willing to learn and try new things. I have been able to do so, through a variety of different internships. My first internship was working as a Lepidopterist at the Butterfly Pavilion. Not only did I manage all stages of butterflies, but I worked on an independent research project throughout my time there. I then moved on to working with Save Our Youth. I was an Education Intern there supporting disadvantaged youth with their educational and emotional skills. I also got the chance to lead a skills activity about computer science. And most recently I interned at Tolstoy, where I lived in Vienna and worked with a small team in a software developing job. As the team grew, they offered me a remote position working part-time to allow me to complete my studies while continuing being a part of their team. All of these past experiences have equipped me with a vast skill set which I am excited to share.

If I am not working, I am either reading, cooking, or spending time with my friends. I currently finished reading Stranger Care by Sarah Sentilles. If you would like to get to know me more or send me any good recipes, feel free to reach out to me at alexcscarola@gmail.com.
