Deep learning is a new and emerging technology that is becoming more prevelant in our daily lives. The first slide deck contains information on deep learning for an audience with no background knowledge in technology, the second slide deck is a technical introduction to deep learning, and the third slide deck is the look into the future of deep learning. All of this is also accompianed with a research paper, that should portray all of the information on my slide decks and presentations in a organized formate.
