My Various Projects

Bufflists

A team and I created Bufflists which is a mock lists of an ecommerce site. This website allows University of Colorado students to be able to buy and sell different products and services. The different components this website contains are login, sign up, home page, create a listing, your listings, account settings, and preference settings pages. This website is functional on local databases.

Cocktail Recipes

For this project I created a website that allowed you to search up different cocktails by name. For each individual cocktail it presented if the cocktail is alcoholic, the glass type, and the instructions. You also have the option to add a review, and look at previous reviews and when they were made.
